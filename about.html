About

2018

In April, I made the ambitious leap of becoming a self-employed makeup artist. After becoming self-employed, I developed a large client base, whilst renting a work station within a local salon.

2019

On the 10th February, I was awarded the Best Makeup Artist in Fife at the Fife Retail Business Awards, held at the Keavil Hotel in Crossford. This was an amazing achievement, and it qualified me for the Scottish Business Awards in May.

Winner of the Best Makeup Artist in Fife at The Fife Retail Business Awards 2019

On the 19th May, I was awarded the Best Makeup Artist in Scotland at the Scottish Business Awards, held at the Glasgow Marriott Hotel. An incredible achievement that I was able to share with family & friends.

Winner of the Best Makeup Artist in Scotland at The Scottish Business Awards 2019

In July, I travelled abroad for my first international wedding, held on the beautiful Greek island of Santorini. This was a magical experience and every day I'm grateful to be able to experience opportunities like this.

My first experience of working abroad.

In November, I achieved a milestone in my career of opening my very own salon. Having a place to call my own was something I've always wanted and it's such an amazing feeling. Having my own space where my clients feel comfortable and happy is what I've always dreamed of.

My beautiful salon

2020

In March, I performed my first masterclass at Perth College. This was an amazing experience and something I would love to be involved with again in the future.

My first masterclass at Perth College

In August, I achieved one of my lifelong dreams of launching my own lash brand, Infinite Lashes.

Available at www.infinite-lashes.co.uk

My own lash brand, a lifelong dream.
